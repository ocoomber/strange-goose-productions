Track record

Award-winning.

Strange Goose is a three-person film team — director Owen Coomber, producer Torya Winters, and cinematographer Alex McNeill. Based in Edinburgh, operating since 2024.

Our 48-hour competition track record — seven entries, wins and nominations across every single one — demonstrates the ability to produce quality work under extreme pressure, with no budget, to a hard deadline.

That's the same thing a commercial brief needs. A tight timeline, a fixed budget, and a result that has to be good.
