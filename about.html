Strange Goose Productions

A small team.
Serious work.

We're a three-person film production company based in Edinburgh. Director, producer, cinematographer. We've been making things together since 2024.

Not an agency.
Not a bedroom
operation.

Strange Goose Productions was founded in Edinburgh in 2024. We make short films, music videos, and branded commercial content for clients who want something that looks like a real film — not a content package.

We work at the intersection of traditional filmmaking craft and a lean modern pipeline that lets us move faster and spend less than a conventional production company — without sacrificing the quality of the result.

Our 48-hour competition track record isn't a party trick. It's evidence of something that matters to clients: we can produce genuinely good work under extreme time and budget pressure. That's the same skill set every production job requires.

"Short film. Music video. Brand commercial. Award-winning, whatever the deadline."
