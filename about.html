How we got
good at this.

We started by making horror shorts nobody paid us for. On fifty quid. For the fun of it.

One of those shorts went on to win international festival awards. On top of that we've made short films as part of a 48hr film making challenge and won multiple awards as well as the entire competition for the city of Aberdeen. The lessons we learned - what you actually need vs. what productions pretend you need, how to make one shoot day produce multiple deliverables, how to hit a deadline - have turned into a way of working.

Now, when a brand comes to us with a real-world budget and a tight timeline, we already know how to make it work. Because we've been making it work for ourselves for years.
