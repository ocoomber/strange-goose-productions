Who we are

Not an agency.
Not a bedroom.
A film team.

Strange Goose is a small, experienced production team based in Edinburgh. We've been making films — real ones, with crews, on locations, under pressure — for years. We know how to direct, how to shoot, how to cut, and how to make something that actually holds an audience.

We've also built a production pipeline that lets us move faster and work leaner than a traditional company. For brands that need quality without a six-figure budget and a three-month timeline, that's a meaningful difference.
