How it works

The process

You don't need to
know anything about video.

Most people who contact us have never commissioned a video before. That's fine. Our job is to make this easy — not to impress you with industry language.

01

One conversation

Tell us what you're selling and what you're trying to achieve. That's it. We ask the questions. No briefs to fill in, no jargon to navigate.

02

We develop the idea

We come back to you with a creative concept, a storyboard, and a fixed price. Nothing gets made until you've seen the plan and signed off on the cost.

03

We shoot it

Our team — director, producer, cinematographer — handles the entire production day. You can be on set or not. Your call.

04

You get the files

We edit and deliver every format and length you need, named by platform and ready to upload. Instagram Reel. YouTube pre-roll. TikTok. Stories. All of it.

What you receive

Every version you need.
Nothing you have to figure out.

The files arrive named, sized, and ready. You don't need to know what 9:16 means or what a pre-roll is. We handle that.

Formats

Every screen ratio

Vertical for Instagram and TikTok. Square for feed posts. Widescreen for YouTube. All cut from the same shoot — no extra cost, no extra day.

Lengths

Short, shorter, shortest

60 seconds for the full story. 30 seconds for an ad break. 10 seconds for a quick hit. Each one edited as its own piece — not just a trim.

Labelling

Named by platform

Files arrive clearly named: "Instagram_Reel_60s", "YouTube_PreRoll_30s", "TikTok_15s". Open the folder. Upload the file. Done.

Timeline

Weeks, not months

From first conversation to finished files, we typically turn projects around in two to four weeks. We'll give you an honest estimate upfront — and we won't disappear for three months.

Track record

Strange Goose is a three-person film team based in Edinburgh — director Owen Coomber, producer Torya Winters, and cinematographer Alex McNeill. We make short films, music videos, and commercial content.

Our short film Fairytale Farm — shot for £50 — won five festival awards and was selected for the Lift-Off Global Sessions. Our 48-hour competition films have accumulated wins and nominations across seven competitions in the past two years.

We tell you this not to impress you, but to make a practical point: we know how to get exceptional results on limited budgets, under real pressure, to a deadline. That's exactly what a commercial production needs to be.
